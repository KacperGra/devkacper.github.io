DunHero is a fantasy action roguelite where each run lets you combine new items, classes, and upgrades to create powerful builds. Explore changing worlds, fight intense bosses, and jump into co-op sessions with friends.

• 600+ items to discover

• 28 playable classes

• 80+ level-up cards

• 8 worlds with unique enemies and bosses

• Solo or 2-4 player co-op
